MATRIX

A computer hacker learns from mysterious rebels about the true nature of his reality and his role in the war against its controllers.

Storyline:

Thomas A. Anderson is a man living two lives. By day he is an average computer programmer and by night a hacker known as Neo. Neo has always questioned his reality, but the truth is far beyond his imagination. Neo finds himself targeted by the police when he is contacted by Morpheus, a legendary computer hacker branded a terrorist by the government. As a rebel against the machines, Neo must confront the agents: super-powerful computer programs devoted to stopping Neo and the entire human rebellion.

Genres:

Action | Sci-Fi

Premiere:

The Matrix was first introduced on March 31, 1999. It had revenue of $ 171 million in North America, over $ 292 million in various foreign ticket offices, bringing in $ 463 million worldwide. Later, the first DVD was sold in more than three million copies in the United States. The Ultimate Matrix Collection was unveiled on May 22, 2007 on HD DVD and on October 14, 2008 on Blu-ray.The film was unveiled in its 10th anniversary release on Blu-ray in Digibook format on March 31, 2009, 10 years after the film’s unveiling.
